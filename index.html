Our Transparency Promise

We believe in complete transparency. RemoteIndia is a matching service that connects job seekers with employers. We do not:

  • Guarantee employment or specific income levels
  • Charge any fees to job seekers
  • Promise "easy money" or get-rich-quick schemes
  • Request payment or sensitive financial information

Actual job opportunities, requirements, and compensation are determined solely by employers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service.

Is there any fee to use your service?
No. Our service is completely free for job seekers. We are compensated by companies who use our platform to find qualified remote workers.
How long does it take to find a remote position?
The timeline varies based on your skills, experience, and current market demand. Some find opportunities within days, while for others it may take several weeks. We encourage you to be patient and persistent in your search.
What kind of remote jobs are available?
We work with companies across various industries including technology, customer service, healthcare, education, marketing, and more. Positions range from entry-level to executive roles.
How will companies contact me?
Employers may contact you via email or phone using the contact information you provide. We never share your information without your consent, and you can opt out at any time.
Do you guarantee I'll find a job?
We cannot guarantee employment. Our role is to connect you with potential employers. The hiring decision ultimately rests with the companies reviewing your application.
